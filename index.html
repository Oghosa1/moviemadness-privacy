Privacy Policy for MovieMadness

Effective date: September 2025

MovieMadness (“the App”) is developed by GuerrillaDev. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used only to create your account and display your profile within the app. We do not store this data on our servers.
  • AdMob (Google Ads): We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device information, IP address, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is handled in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used only for:

  • Authenticating users via Google Sign-In.
  • Displaying ads through Google AdMob.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store user data on our own servers. Any data collected through Google services (Sign-In or AdMob) is managed by Google.

If you wish to request deletion of your Google account data from MovieMadness, please contact us at guerrilladev@gmail.com. You may also follow the steps outlined on our Data Deletion Page.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only shared with Google services (Sign-In and AdMob) as described above.
