Data Deletion Request

Effective date: September 2025

At MovieMadness, we respect your privacy and provide an option to request deletion of your account data.

What Data We Hold

MovieMadness does not store personal data on its own servers. Any user information (name, email, profile picture) is provided by Google Sign-In and handled directly by Google.

How to Request Deletion

If you would like your Google Sign-In account data to be deleted from MovieMadness:

  1. Send an email to guerrilladev@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the Google account email address you used to sign in.
  3. We will process your request and ensure your data is no longer accessible in the app.
