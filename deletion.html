Data Deletion Request

Effective date: September 2025

At MovieMadness, we respect your privacy and provide an option to request deletion of your account data in line with applicable regulations.

What Data We Hold

MovieMadness does not store personal data on its own servers. Any user information (such as your name, email, and profile picture) is provided by Google Sign-In and handled directly by Google. We only use this data within the app to create your account and display your profile.

How to Request Deletion

If you would like your Google Sign-In account data to be deleted from MovieMadness:

  1. Send an email to guerrilladev@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the Google account email address you used to sign in.
  3. We will verify your request and confirm receipt within 48 hours.
  4. Once verified, your request will be fully processed within 7 business days. During this time, your access to the app’s features linked to your Google account may be restricted.
  5. After completion, we will send you a confirmation email stating that your data is no longer accessible in MovieMadness.

Third-Party Services

Data handled by Google Sign-In and AdMob is managed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies. For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.
